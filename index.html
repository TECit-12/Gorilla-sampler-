Professional Features

🎹

16 Pad Sampler

Professional 4x4 pad interface with velocity sensitivity, LED feedback, and customizable colors. Touch responsiveness optimized for live performance.

🎼

Step Sequencer

16/32/64 step patterns with 128+ pattern banks. Real-time editing, swing/shuffle controls, and pattern chaining for song composition.

🎚️

Audio Effects

Real-time filters (Low-pass, High-pass, Band-pass), reverb, delay, compression, EQ, and distortion with parametric control.

⚡

Ultra-Low Latency

Sub-20ms latency achieved through native C++ audio engine with Oboe API. Professional-grade audio processing for real-time responsiveness.

🎙️

Multi-Track Recording

Record performances with multiple modes: Replace, Mix, Loop Record. Export to WAV, MP3, and AAC formats with lossless quality options.

💾

Sample Management

Import, edit, and organize samples. Waveform editing, pitch shift, time stretch, and drag-and-drop pad assignment. Cloud backup support.
